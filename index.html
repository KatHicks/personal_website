About

I'm a young professional embarking on a career in software development and have spent the last two years working as a map-maker at the British Red Cross. I've got degrees in Geography and Art & Design, and spend a lot of time teaching myself things too.

I'm genuinely interested in most things and am currently keen on open data, data visualisation and climbing.

I'm keen to connect with people working on similar things, receive feedback on my work and hear about any relevant job openings. If you have any questions or comments, or have an opening that you think would suit me, please get in touch!
